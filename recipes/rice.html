White Rice

How to cook white rice

White rice is a simple and filling dish to get you full and satisfied with little trouble.

Ingredients

  • White Rice

Steps

  1. Add rice to a medium size pot.
  2. Add water.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Bring to a boil.
  5. Drop to a simmer
  6. Cover.
  7. Cook for 18 minutes.
  8. Let sit for five minutes.
  9. Fluff with a fork.
