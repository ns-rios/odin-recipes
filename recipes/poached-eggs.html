Poached Eggs

How to poach an egg

Poached eggs are one of the best ways for anyone to enjoy this delicious dish. Able to be cooked in a short period of time this dish is one of my favorites and one I would recommend to anyone to enjoy.
  • Egg
  • White Vineger
  1. Boil a medium pot of water.
  2. Add a table spoon of white vineger.
  3. Crack an egg into a strainer over a ramiken.
  4. Once water is boiling, swirl the water into a whirl.
  5. Slide egg into the water.
  6. Cook for three minutes.
  7. Remove the egg with a slotted spoon.
  8. Pat drive with a paper towel.
