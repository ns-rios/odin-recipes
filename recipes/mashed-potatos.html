Mashed Potatoes

How to make mashed potatoes

Mashed potatoes are a quick and easy meal for when you want something filling a fast.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb. potatoes, cut into quarters
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large saucepan; add enough water to cover.
  2. Boil 15 to 20 minutes and drain.
  3. Return potatoes to saucepan; shake pan over low heat for 1 to 2 minutes to evaporate any excess moisture.
  4. Mash potatoes with potato masher until no lumps remain.
  5. Add butter and salt continue mashing gradually adding milk until smooth and creamy.
